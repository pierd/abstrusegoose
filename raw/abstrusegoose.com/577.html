Prime Porkour


click or tap to jump
(helpful hint: you can reverse gravity.)

NOTE: I noticed that this game sometimes seems to be laggy. For a slightly less laggy version,
you can download the Android version from Google Play (it's free).

I would consider a score of 3000 to be good. 6000 is superb. If you can finish this game, you are superhuman!
